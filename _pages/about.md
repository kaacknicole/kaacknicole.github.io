<p>Nicole Kaack is a writer, editor, curator, and educator whose research interests include language, memory, and performance.</p>
  
<p>Kaack's writing has been published by <i>Art in America</i>, the <i>Brooklyn Rail</i>, <i>ARTnews</i>, <i>Artforum</i>, <i>ArtReview</i>, <i>BOMB</i>, <i>Cleveland Review of Books</i>, and <i>Sound American</i>. She has organized exhibitions and programs at the Kitchen, Hunter College, A.I.R. Gallery, NARS, Miriam Gallery, HESSE FLATOW, and Small Editions. Previous roles include editorial program manager at the Institute for Studies on Latin American Art (ISLAA), associate director at A.I.R. Gallery, curatorial fellow at The Kitchen, and archival fellow at the Museum of Modern Art, New York.</p> 

<p>With <a href="https://cargocollective.com/miradayal" target="_blank">Mira Dayal</a>, Kaack is co-founder of the collaborative artists' book project <a href="https://cargocollective.com/promptcolon" target="_blank"><i>prompt:</i></a>.</p>
